Description

"Money Movers Maker" is a fun and engaging game for kids where players get to create their own levels and challenges for the Money Movers characters. The objective is to think creatively and strategically to design levels that are both challenging and entertaining for other players to complete.

To play "Money Movers Maker," kids can use their imagination to build obstacles, puzzles, and traps for the Money Movers characters to navigate through. They can also test their levels to ensure they are challenging and fun before sharing them with friends or playing other levels created by the community. This game encourages critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity in a playful and interactive way.
